About

Halli-Galli is one of the card games which was made by Amigo in Germany. It consists of a bell with a switch and cards with pictures of fruits. The advantage is the cute card with pictures of fruits and rules that anyone can easily understand! Although it has simple rules, it is said to be an excellent board game because it embodied all kinds of fights, tension, and nervousness.

Designer

Haim Shafir (born c. 1950) is a game designer and mechanical engineer from Haifa, Israel, who is arguably Israel's most prolific game designer. After two years as a mechanical engineer, he was drawn to the magic of toys and in 1978 he started his journey in the toy industry as a designer of wooden toys. Later he shifted my interest to games, focusing on family games which appeal to all ages. Now, he is a game inventor and CEO of Shafir Games. Through the years he has developed over 100 games which are sold in 32 countries. Some of my most famous games are Taki and Halli-Galli.
